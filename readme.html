Introduction

 

Kenny and Spenny are two best friends who compete against each other. Their competitions are ridiculous, immature and totally intense.

 

Kenny: A witty wise-cracker and brilliant schemer. Kenny's a goofball looking for a shortcut. He loves to humiliate Spenny and will do anything to win.

 

Spenny: A stressed out, neurotic, short-fused adult-type who believes in preparation and hard work. He's responsible, often angry, introspective, paranoid and ethical.

 

Why do they constantly compete? No one's sure but it's a fact that they are a pathetic example of the male species! So, if you want to be like them, we suggest you immediately contact a psychiatrist.

 

Now you can compete against them or become them (well virtually of course) in Versusville.

 

In Versusville you can do battle in four exciting games.

 

            Residence Evil

New arrivals to Versusville must first play Residence Evil. This game is only presented in the single-player mode and is the tutorial for all the other games.

 

Wasteball

An abandoned building has been transformed into a chemical waste paintball arena, where players compete in brutal shooting matches.

 

Kart Dart

Competitors are challenged to race with shopping trolleys around the Versus Food Market, they have 3 tracks to choose from and battle their way to the front of the pack, by fair means or foul, and cross the finish line first with the most competition points.

Grave Rave

A cemetery late at night is the perfect place for peer humiliation. Players must use a gruesome array of weapons to dispatch the other players but they do not shoot�they throw!  And they launch pieces of exploded zombie!

Running the Game

 

When navigating the Menus, keep in mind that all menu screens have a Back Button.

 

Main Menu

 

The main menu consists of the following:

 

            Wasteball and Grave Rave

           

                        Single Player

                       

Select number of opponents you would like to battle with and then chose your winning conditions (number of frags and time limit). When you are ready click on the Ok Button to start the game.

 

                        Multiplayer

 

From this menu you have the option to either Join or Host a game.

 

If you simply wish to join a game select the desired game from the server list and click the Ok Button.

 

If you wish to host a game click on the Host Button located at the top of the screen. A private game will require you to enter a password that will block others from joining your game unless they have your password. A public game allows anyone to join your game.

 

Select the desired number of frags and time limit then click Ok and you are ready to battle.

Instructions

 

                        When this button is clicked the instructions for the game are displayed.

 

Kart Dart

 

                        Single Player

 

Select the number of opponents you would like to race against, track type (3 race tracks are available) and how many laps you wish to race for. When done click Ok and start racing!

 

                        Multiplayer

 

From this menu you have the option to either Join or Host a game.

 

If you simply wish to join a game select the desired game from the server list and click the Ok Button.

 

If you wish to host a game click on the Host Button located at the top of the screen. A private game will require you to enter a password that will block others from joining your game unless they have your password. A public game allows anyone to join your game.

 

Select the track type and how many laps you wish to race for. When done click Ok to begin the race.

 

                        Instructions

 

                        When this button is clicked the instructions for the game are displayed.

 

            Residence Evil

 

Simply click the Ok Button and you will be sneaking around Kenny and Spenny�s place in no time.

 

            KvS TV Repair Shop

 

Using this button will bring you to the official Kenny vs, Spenny web site - http://www.cbc.ca/kennyvsspenny/

 

            Hall of Champions

 

This button will open the High Score Tables web site - http://www.cbc.ca/kennyvsspenny/scores

 

                        Your Versusville score will be displayed here for all to see!

 

            War Memorial

 

                        This is the area of Versusville where all your war medals are displayed.

 

The medal case is divided into two sections - the Master medals and the Slave medals. There are 32 medals to be won in all. There are two spots at the top of the case, where if you succeed in winning all the Master medals you will receive the key to Versusville, BUT if you receive all the Slave medals you get a bronzed pair of Kenny�s underpants.

 

            Option Alley

 

This is the location where the player can change many of the main game options. 

 

An options window will appear giving access to the following game parameters:

 

Game - Here you can change the mouse speed, invert the mouse, toggle left-handed controls and select a crosshair.

 

Video - Screen resolution, texture options, toggle full-screen and driver information are found here.

 

Audio - This is the area where you enable/disable sounds and adjust the volume for ambient sound as well as sound effects.

 

Profile - It�s you! Change your name, character and costume here.

 

Credits - View the credits of who was behind the game.

 

            Quit

 

                        Exit Versusville and return to windows.

Updating video drivers on your system

First determine what type of video card you have. To do this:

 

1.       Click Start

2.       Click Run

3.       In the box type DXDIAG. Click OK. This will bring up the DirectX Diagnostic Tool

4.       When the tool finishes loading, click the Display tab.

 

In the Device section, next to Name is the name of your video card. The Manufacturer is the company that manufactured your video card.

The best place to get the latest driver is the manufacturer�s web site.


Follow the instructions on the manufacturer's web site to download and install the latest drivers.

Update the sound drivers on your system

 

First determine what type of sound card you have. To do this:

 

1.       Click Start

2.       Click Run

3.       In the box type DXDIAG

4.       Click OK. This will bring up the DirectX Diagnostic Tool

5.       When the tool finishes loading, click the Sound tab.

 

In the Device section, next to Name is the name of your device. The Manufacturer is the company that manufactured your sound card.

The best place to get the latest driver is the manufacturer�s web site.

Disable all 3rd party applications running in the background

 

To do this with Win98 and WinME:

1.       Hold down the Ctrl+Alt+Delete keys simultaneously on your keyboard.

2.       This will bring up the Close Program window.

This window will show all of the programs running currently on your system. We want to temporarily close all of these applications except for Explorer and Systray. To close these other programs:

3.       Click once to highlight the first program listed.

4.       Hit the End Task button

5.       Now repeat steps 1-4 to remove all other applications. Be careful not to hit Ctrl+Alt+Delete twice or it will cause your computer to reboot. Your computer will also reboot if you close Explorer or Systray or hit the Shut Down button.

 

To do this with Win2000 and WinXP:

1.       Hold down the Ctrl+Alt+Delete keys simultaneously on your keyboard.

2.       Click the Task Manager button.

3.       Click the Applications tab. This will list programs currently running on your system

4.       Hold down the Ctrl key on the keyboard and click each program to highlight it.

5.       When all the programs are highlighted, hit the End Task button. If you get any messages that state the program is not responding, hit End Task again.

6.       Now click the Processes tab. Click the bar that says Image Name to sort the list.

7.       Click on any of the processes except EXPLORER and TASKMGR that have your login name in the Image Name column. Then click End Process. Click Yes on the Task Manager Warning. Repeat this step to remove all processes that are under your login name except EXPLORER and TASKMGR.

 

This should temporarily close any conflicting background applications. No permanent changes are being made. These applications will start up again when you reboot.

 

Update the DirectX version on your system

 

To update DirectX:

 

1.       You will need to go to http://www.microsoft.com/directx

2.       Look for a DirectX download link

3.       Select the appropriate download for your version of Windows

4.       Follow the instructions on the Microsoft site for proper installation.

 

Make more hard drive space available

 

Run disk cleanup:

 

1.       Double-click the My Computer icon on your desktop

2.       Hold down the Alt key on the keyboard and double-click the hard drive icon

3.       On the General tab, click the Disk Cleanup button

4.       In Disk Cleanup put a check in the boxes next to Temporary Internet Files, Recycle Bin, and Temporary Files. Make sure other boxes are not checked

5.      Click OK. A message will ask you if you want to perform these actions. Click Yes. This will clear out your temporary directories and empty your Recycle Bin

 

Firewall and network connections

 

If you cannot connect or otherwise join/host a multiplayer game please verify the following:

 

1.       If you have a firewall you can either open ports 5200 and 5500 or disable the firewall for the duration of your game. For further information on ports and disabling your firewall please refer to your firewall user manual.

 

For WinXP users, XP has a built in firewall. To disable it:

 

- First, if applicable, get your parents permission, and write yourself a reminder to turn the firewall back on once you are finished playing

- Click on the Start button

- Move the mouse to Settings

- Then to Control Panel

- Switch to category view if it is not already

- Right click on the connection and select properties

- Select the advanced tab

- Uncheck the box labeled "internet connection firewall"

 

Note: Make sure that you recheck this box once you're done playing!

 

2.       That all your network connections are working properly

3.       Verify that your Internet connection is working properly by opening a webpage

 

Username on different computers

 

The username that you created on your computer can only be used on that computer. If you install the game on a different computer you will be required to create a new username.

 

Naming a private multiplayer game

 

Make sure that you name your private multiplayer game with a minimum of 3 characters.
